What Auralethi Is

Practical support tools for people who need systems designed around how they actually work.

Auralethi is a nonprofit project developing practical support tools for people who struggle with memory, organization, executive function, disability, or cognitive overload.

Current work includes:

  • Designing the Hollarin AI Companion
  • Developing the internal CortexOS architecture
  • Preparing funding and grant proposals
  • Expanding organizational structure
  • Documenting progress publicly
  • Defining the long-term roadmap

CortexOS is the internal architecture being designed to support future AI tools and is not itself a public product.

This phase focuses on building the foundation correctly before moving to prototypes.

Long-Term Direction

Building environments that make life easier to live.

The long-term goal of Auralethi is to create tools, programs, and environments that make life easier to live for people who need support that current systems do not provide.

This includes:

  • Ethical AI companions
  • Support-focused software
  • Cooperative community environments
  • Accessible living spaces

Valenhold represents a future phase of this vision.

The current focus is building the foundation that makes that future possible.
